The world we live in today is suffering environmental problems,
like air pollution and climate change, and it continues to worsen
by the day. With the continuing degradation of our environment,
it is up to us to help with its protection and what better place
there is to start than our very own homes.

This is encouraged by Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur.
The Barangay prohibits burning any kind of material within
the barangay for it causes air pollution and it can contribute
to the ever present problem of Global Warming.

Dealing with garbage is also strict in the barangay.
Throwing your garbage in canals or leaving them on the
streets is not allowed. They also encourage households to
segregate their garbage because they have the “No segregation,
no collection” policy where garbage will not be collected if
they are not segregated.

Recycling is also encouraged in the barangay.
Recycling is the act of transforming waste material
into something new, something that can be used again.
Recycling is part of the 3 R’s, Reuse, Reduce, and Recycle.
The 3 R’s aims to reduce to the amount of waste that is
thrown by simply reusing waste materials that could still be used,
reducing consumption of materials so there would be less waste materials,
and recycling waste materials.

The barangay also works with the neighboring university,
Saint Louis University Maryheights Campus, to conduct its
annual tree planting programs. Trees are beneficial to
the protection of the environment especially here in Baguio City.
Trees help in dealing with the inevitable and evident air pollution
in the city brought by toxic gases released by thousands of cars in the city.
