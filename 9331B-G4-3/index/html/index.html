Environmental Protection

The world we live in today is suffering environmental problems, like air pollution and climate
change, and it continues to worsen by the day. With the continuing degradation of our environment,
it is up to us to help with its protection and what better place there is to start than our very own homes.



This is encouraged by Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur. The Barangay prohibits burning any kind
of material within the barangay for it causes air pollution and it can contribute to the
ever present problem of Global Warming.

Dealing with garbage is also strict in the barangay. Throwing your garbage in canals or
leaving them on the streets is not allowed. They also encourage households to segregate their
garbage because they have a "No segregation, No collection" policy where garbages will not
be collected if they are not segregated.



Recycling is also encouraged in the barangay. Recycling is the act of transforming waste
into something new, something that can be used again. Recycling is part of the 3 R's, Reduce,
Reuse, and Recycle. The 3 R's aims to reduce the amount of waste materials that couls still
be used, reducing consumption of materials so there would be less waste materials, and
recycling waste materials.

The barangay also works with the neighboring university, Saint Louis University Maryheights
Campus, to conduct its annual tree planting programs. Trees are beneficial to the protection
of the environment especially here in Baguio City. Trees help in dealing with the inevitable
and evident air pollution in the city brought by by toxic gases released by the thousands
of vehicles in the city.
