About

Situated 10-15 minutes (depending on the traffic) away from Baguio City's main town proper,
Bakakeng resides peacefully surrounded with trees, providing a good environment to put up a
house and start a family. Bakakeng can be accessed from two main highways, Marcos and Kennon
with the former being commonly used by students of the Saint Louis University, built at the
center of the barangay and gives life to Bakakeng.

Vision

We aspire to be a Barangay with Educated and peace-loving
and empowered constituents geared towards a progressive
community.

Mission

Bakakeng Norte/Sur aims at a Collaborative Management and
Collaborative Constituents for and empowered community

Core Values

1. Dedicated to/committed to service.
2. Team work/collaborative leadership in carrying out program of the barangay.
3. God-fearing barangay leaders and constituents.
4. Practice of transparency for good governance.
5. Empowerment od constituents.
6. Leadership skills development.
