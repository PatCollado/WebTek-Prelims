Overview

Baguio City also known as Summer Capital of the Philippines is part of CAR or Cordillera Administrative Region.
It is popular for its cool climate, beautiful flowers and the beautiful people. People from other region visits places like this to
experience the cool climate and foggy hills. Every February, many tourists come to watch the Panagbenga Festival.
Baguio City is bounded on the north by Benguet’s Municipality.
Itogon, Tuba and La Trinidad is also part of Benguet Municipality. Baguio is accessible by land and air travel,
it takes 4-5 hours land travel if you’re from Ilocos and 6 hour travel if you are from Manila.

Bakakeng Norte/Sur is one of the 129 barangays in Baguio City. It currently headed by
their Punong Barangay, Mr. Jessie Jacob Palos Jr. along
with his Barangay Councils:

    Aims

    Our website aims to make Bakakeng Norte/Sur a model who promotes PEACE & ORDER by:

  • encouraging its people to work hand in hand
  • helping its people when in need,
  • providing fairness, justice and generosity,
  • sharing stories and standing up to violence, abuse, rape and bullying,
  • making sure that the Barangay Tanods have a proper training of defense against crimes,
  • making sure that its citizens can sleep peacefully at night,
  • making sure that its citizens can roam freely during daytime,
  • solving problems in an effective way,
  • encouraging its people to smile and be kind always,
  • teaching its people that small gestures of kindness will lead to PEACEFUL and ORDERLY barangay and most of all,
  • making Bakakeng Norte/Sur a stress-free barangay

    Vision

    Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur aspire to be a Barangay with Educated, peace-loving
    and empowered constituents geared towards a progressive community.

    Mission

    Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur aims to be Collaborative Management
    and Cooperative Constituents for an empowered community.
