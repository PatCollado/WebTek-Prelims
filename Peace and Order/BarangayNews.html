Barangay News

Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur to invest additional CCTVs.

Having a CCTV installed is a great help for citizens to roam freely and feel secured at all times. Aside from businesses, barangays are now required to install CCTVs in their respective areas.

Barangay Captain Jessie Jacob Palos Jr. said that Barangay Norte/Sur is strengthening their implementation of peace and order by investing additional CCTVS of the barangay. Currently, the barangay have a total of 8 CCTVs installed. 3 of them are placed in the barangay hall and 5 of them are placed from the barangay hall until the turning point of the barangay. Although this is still in talk because there are other factors to consider first, but Barangay Captain Palos ensures us that he will do his best to pursue this proposal for the safety of the Barangay.

“We need more tanods” – Barangay Captain Jessie Jacob Palos Jr.

>Barangay Tanod or also known as Barangay police officer, Barangay peace and security officer, Barangay peacekeeping and security officer, or Barangay police safety officer may be the lowest level of law enforcement officer in the Philippines but they play a vital role in the development, progress and security of the barangay.

Barangay Bakakeng have a total of 4 tanods. 1 tanod is incharge during morning while 3 tanods are incharge to roam at night. “We really need more tanods” – Barangay Captain Jessie Jacob Palos told us. When we ask the reason why they only have a small number of tanods, he replied “There are no volunteers because everyone is busy here. There are no tambays here. We actually offered them a salary higher than some barangays but there are still no volunteers. “ With this situation, Barangay Captain sometimes asks the Station 10 pulis to help them make the barangay safe especially at night.

Massive Ronda was held to lessen the robbery.

>The primary issue at Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur was robbery which was rampant at subdivisions of the said barangay. Barangay Captain Jessie Jacob Palos Jr held a meeting at District 14 to address the said issue. Different sectors worked together to prevent robbery and other possible issues of the barangay. The different sectors are : Police Station 10, NPO, SWAT and tanods of the same district. With this activity, the robbery was lessened. Thanks to the dedication of the Barangay Officials with the leadership of the Barangay Captain of Bakakeng Norte/Sur, they were able to save lives.Station 10 also offers trainings and seminars to the tanod of the Barangay regarding on how to deal with the possible issues and crimes. They conduct trainings to help maintain and improve the peace and order of the Barangay.

Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur conducts a Neighborhood Watch

10 houses of the Barangay Bakakeng Norte worked together to form a Neighborhood Watch. This was a 24/7 operation. If an unknown or suspicious man was seen roaming around the neighborhood, the houses will communicate together through mobile phones and share information about the possible culprit. This will be a great help for the barangay because even ordinary citizens works together to maintain the peace and order of the barangay.

Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur holds “Monthly Bayanihan”

Bayanihan is what makes a Filipino , Filipino. It is a core essence of the Filipino Culture. It is helping out on neighbor most especially in times of need without expecting anything in return. And this is what the Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur is promoting. Monthly, the barangay holds the “Monthly Bayanihan”. Barangay Captain Jessie Jacob Palos Jr. told us that for example, they conducted a bayanihan cleaning program of purok 8. This has been a great help for the citizen of the said purok. They are also conducting a “Monthly purok meeting” to discuss some issues and improvements for the barangay.
