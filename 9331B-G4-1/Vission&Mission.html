Vision

Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur aspire to be a Barangay with Educated, peace-loving
and empowered constituents geared towards a progressive community.

Mission

Barangay Bakakeng Norte/Sur aims to be Collaborative Management
and Cooperative Constituents for an empowered community.

"If there's no cooperation, there's no Barangay." - Kap Palos Jr.
