Overview

Baguio City also known as Summer Capital of the Philippines is part of CAR or Cordillera Administrative Region.
It is popular for its cool climate, beautiful flowers and the beautiful people. People from other region visits places like this to
experience the cool climate and foggy hills. Every February, many tourists come to watch the Panagbenga Festival.
Baguio City is bounded on the north by Benguet’s Municipality.
Itogon, Tuba and La Trinidad is also part of Benguet Municipality. Baguio is accessible by land and air travel,
it takes 4-5 hours land travel if you’re from Ilocos and 6 hour travel if you are from Manila.
