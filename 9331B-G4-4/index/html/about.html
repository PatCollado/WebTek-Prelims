About

Situated 10-15 minutes (depending on the traffic) away from Baguio City's main town proper,
\ Bakakeng resides peacefully surrounded with trees, providing a good environment to put up a
house and start a family. Bakakeng can be accessed from two main highways, Marcos and Kennon
with the former being commonly used by studentsof the Saint Louis University, built at the
center of the barangay and gives life to Bakakeng.
